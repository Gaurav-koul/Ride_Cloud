# Account and Data Deletion

Users of the **RideCloud** application may request the deletion of their account and associated personal data at any time.

---

## What Data Will Be Deleted

Once your request is verified, the following data will be permanently deleted:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Any account-related information

---

## Data Retention

Some data may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes such as fraud prevention or legal compliance.

---

## Processing Time

Account and data deletion requests are processed within a reasonable timeframe after verification.
